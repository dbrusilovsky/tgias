# TGIAS, LLC - Company Website

Professional company website for TGIAS, LLC, featuring a landing page and 3D model viewer tool.

## About TGIAS

TGIAS, LLC is a multidisciplinary team delivering effective software solutions to cross-domain problems. Based in Beckley, West Virginia, our team consists of engineers, AI specialists, and Human Sciences experts.

We're currently in Phase I of our SBIR-funded research project, developing next-generation tools for infrastructure professionals in partnership with the U.S. Department of Transportation.

### Supported 3D Formats
- GLB (GL Transmission Format Binary)
- GLTF (GL Transmission Format)
